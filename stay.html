Your stay

Everything you need for a comfortable countryside break.

This self-contained apartment is located on the top floor of the host’s farmhouse.

It has its own access at the rear of the building.

Please note that access to the apartment is up a flight of traditional stone steps.

This apartment is suitable for a couple, or a couple with two children, with the option for an additional third child and/or baby by arrangement.

Bedrooms

  • One double bedroom with double bed
  • Two bedside tables, wardrobe, chest of drawers and full-length mirror
  • One single bedroom with bunk beds
  • Pull-out trundle bed available on request
  • Travel cot available on request
  • All bedding provided

Bathroom

  • Modern shower room with contemporary fittings
  • No bath tub
  • Baby bath available on request
  • Towels provided

Living & Dining Area

  • Open-plan lounge, dining and kitchen space
  • Log burning stove
  • 50" TV
  • Corner sofa
  • Dining table for four
  • Highchair available on request
  • Exposed beams and skylight
  • Front door lobby with coat and shoe rack

Kitchen

  • Induction hob, electric oven and grill
  • Microwave, fridge, toaster and kettle
  • Dishwasher and washer/dryer
  • Cooking and dining utensils provided
  • Tea towels and dishcloths provided

Outdoor

  • Small patio area with bistro set for four
  • Parking space for one car
  • Security light
  • Security camera

Additional Information

  • Central heating
  • Wi-Fi
  • Spare pillows and blankets
  • Hairdryer
  • Iron and ironing board
  • Clothes airer and pegs
  • Mop and vacuum cleaner

A few extra touches

Ready for relaxed stays from the moment you arrive.

One well-behaved small to medium dog may be considered by prior arrangement, subject to an additional cleaning charge.
