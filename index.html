Welcome

Set beside a peaceful brook and just a short walk from Marple Bridge.

Tucked into a private farmhouse setting on the edge of the Peak District, The Old Workshop offers a relaxed base for countryside stays with easy access to Manchester.

Original beams, a wood burning stove and thoughtful modern touches give the apartment an easy, comfortable feel throughout.

Sleeps up to 5

Suitable for a couple, or a couple with two children, with the option for an additional third child and/or baby by arrangement.

Cosy open-plan living

Log burning stove, 50" TV, corner sofa, exposed beams and a bright, welcoming feel throughout.

Practical and well equipped

Fully equipped kitchen, shower room, Wi-Fi, central heating, patio and parking for one car.

Your stay

Private, peaceful and easy to settle into.

This self-contained apartment is located on the top floor of the host’s farmhouse and has its own access at the rear of the building via a flight of traditional stone steps.

  • One double bedroom and one single bedroom with bunk beds
  • Pull-out trundle bed and travel cot available on request
  • Modern shower room with towels provided
  • Fully equipped kitchen with dishwasher and washer/dryer
  • Small patio area with bistro set and parking for one car
  • One small to medium dog considered by prior arrangement
